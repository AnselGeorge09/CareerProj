Projected Outcome and Salary for Software Developers

Job Outlook

Employment opportunities for software developers are projected to grow 26% from 2022 to 2032, much faster than the average for all occupations.

Median Annual Salary

When it comes to the financial side of being a software developer, things look pretty good! As of May 2023, the median annual salary for software developers was around $132,270. That’s not too shabby, right? This means that half of all developers make more than that amount, and many experienced pros can earn even more. The great thing is that salaries can vary based on where you live, your level of experience, and the specific tech stack you work with. For instance, developers in tech hubs like Silicon Valley or New York City often see higher pay due to the cost of living and demand for talent. Overall, if you’re considering a career in software development, you can expect a solid paycheck along with plenty of opportunities for growth and advancement in your career!

Salary Range

Salaries can vary widely based on experience, location, and company. Entry-level positions may start around $78,000, while experienced developers can earn over $200,000 annually.
